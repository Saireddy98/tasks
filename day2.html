Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken (or vegetables)
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 cup curd
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tbsp ginger–garlic paste
  • Oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Mint & coriander leaves
  • Whole spices (cardamom, cloves, bay leaf)

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 20–30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil, add whole spices and sliced onions. Fry until golden.
  3. Add ginger–garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  4. Add chicken (or vegetables) and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add tomatoes, curd, biryani masala, mint, and coriander.
  6. Cook until the chicken becomes soft.
  7. Boil the soaked rice separately until 70% cooked.
  8. Layer rice over the chicken gravy in the pot.
  9. Cover and cook on low flame for 15–20 minutes (dum).
  10. Mix gently and serve hot.
